How to Compress Images Without Losing Quality

Need to send images faster, upload to websites, or save storage? Our Image Compressor tool helps you shrink file sizes while keeping your pictures looking sharp.

Steps to Compress

  1. Go to the Image Compressor page.
  2. Click Choose Images and select one or more files.
  3. Use the Quality slider to choose the desired compression level:
    • 70–80% = Great balance for most photos
    • 50–60% = Smaller files, minor quality loss
  4. Click Compress & Download to save the results.

Why Compress?

  • Reduce upload times for websites and social media.
  • Save storage space on devices and cloud services.
  • Meet email attachment limits without splitting files.

Pro Tips

  • Batch select multiple files to save time.
  • Combine compression with resizing for maximum size reduction.
  • Use JPG for photos and PNG/WebP for graphics with transparency.
