JPG vs PNG vs WebP vs AVIF: Which Image Format Should You Use?

Choosing the right format can cut file size by 50–80% with no visible loss. Here’s a quick rule-of-thumb table:

Use CaseBest FormatWhy
Photos/people/sceneryJPG or WebPExcellent compression for gradients & real-world detail
Logos/icons with transparencyPNG or WebPSharp edges, supports transparency
Screenshots/UIWebP or PNGCrisp text and shapes
Cutting-edge performanceAVIF (fallback to WebP/JPG)Smallest sizes but not supported everywhere

Practical recommendations

  • Export JPG/WebP at ~75–85% quality for most photos.
  • Keep PNG for transparency or pixel-perfect edges.
  • Use AVIF only if your audience is on modern browsers (set fallbacks).
