Quick Background Removal: Click + Tolerance (No Upload)

Our Background Remover deletes simple backgrounds in seconds—right in your browser for privacy.

How It Works

  1. Choose an image and it appears on a checkerboard canvas.
  2. Click a background region to pick its color.
  3. Adjust the Tolerance slider to remove more/less similar shades.
  4. Click Download PNG to save with transparency.

Best Results

  • Use photos with a plain background (white/gray/solid colors).
  • Click multiple background areas (corners) and raise tolerance to catch shadows.
  • If edges look rough, try resizing the image down first on the homepage.
