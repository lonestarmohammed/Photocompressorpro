Create a Multi-Page PDF from Photos (iPhone & Android)

Updated: October 18, 2025 • 4–6 min read

Need to send homework scans, receipts, or ID photos as one document? Merge photos into a single, multi-page PDF—free and private—right in your browser.

Step-by-step

  1. Open the Image → PDF tool on your phone.
  2. Tap Choose Images and select your photos.
  3. Pick A4 or Letter, set Margin to 10–15mm.
  4. Use Contain to avoid cropping; Cover to fill the page.
  5. Tap Create PDF. On iPhone, the PDF may open in a new tab—use Share → Save to Files.

Make it smaller (optional)

Compress images first for a smaller PDF:

  1. Open the Image Compressor.
  2. Set Quality to 70–80% and download.
  3. Now convert with Image → PDF.
Tip: Photograph documents in good lighting and keep the phone parallel for sharp text.

Common questions

  • Uploads? No—processing happens locally in your browser.
  • PNG + JPG? Yes, you can mix formats in one PDF.
  • Page order? Select images in the final order; (drag-reorder UI coming soon).
