Mobile Workflow

Scan Documents With Your Phone and Convert to PDF (Free & Fast)

Need to send notes or receipts as a single PDF? Snap photos with your phone and merge them right in your browser—no app installs, no watermark.

Capture better pages

  • Shoot in bright, even light; avoid shadows.
  • Fill the frame and keep the camera parallel to the page.
  • Use “Document” mode in your camera app if available.

Create a single PDF (Step-by-step)

  1. Open the Image → PDF tool.
  2. Tap Choose Images and select your photos (in order).
  3. Pick A4 or Letter, set Margins to ~10mm.
  4. Use Contain fit to avoid cropping.
  5. Tap Create PDF to download (on iPhone it may open in a new tab to save).
Tip: If the PDF is large, compress the photos first on the homepage.
