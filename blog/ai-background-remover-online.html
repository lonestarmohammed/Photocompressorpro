Removing backgrounds from photos used to require Photoshop or hours of editing. Now, with our AI Background Remover, you can erase any photo background instantly—directly in your browser. No uploads, no watermarks, and completely free.

Why use an AI background remover?

  • E-commerce ready: Clean, white backgrounds boost sales on Amazon, eBay, Etsy, and Shopify.
  • Social media: Create standout profile photos, thumbnails, and stories in seconds.
  • Design flexibility: Export transparent PNGs for use in Canva, PowerPoint, or Figma.

How it works

  1. Open the Background Remover.
  2. Click Choose Image and select your photo.
  3. Click a background area to pick the color, then adjust Tolerance to remove similar shades.
  4. Use Auto-pick Corner to sample all four corners quickly.
  5. Click Download PNG to save with transparency.
Pro tip: For complex subjects (hair, glass), try 2–3 clicks on different background spots and increase tolerance gradually.

Best results checklist

  • Use photos with plain backgrounds (white/gray/solid colors).
  • Avoid heavy motion blur; sharp edges are detected more cleanly.
  • Resize large images first on the Image Compressor for faster processing.
