How to Convert Images to a Single PDF (Free & Private)

Need to email multiple photos as one PDF? Our Image → PDF tool runs fully in your browser—nothing is uploaded, so it’s fast and private.

Steps

  1. Open the Image → PDF tool and tap Choose Images.
  2. Select JPG/PNG files (they will appear in the preview grid).
  3. Pick a Page size (A4 or Letter) and Orientation.
  4. Set a Margin (10–15mm works well).
  5. Choose a Fit mode:
    • Contain – no cropping (best for documents)
    • Cover – fills the page (may crop)
    • Fit width/height – match one dimension exactly
  6. Click Create PDF to download (on iPhone it may open in a new tab—use “Share → Save to Files”).

Tips for Smaller PDFs

  • Compress images on the homepage before converting.
  • Use Contain and keep margins around 10mm.
  • Prefer JPG photos over PNG (unless you need transparency).
