Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

At PhotoCompressorPro, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Our tools are designed with privacy as a core principle - your images are processed directly in your browser and never uploaded to our servers.

Information We Collect

No Image Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your images to any external servers. All image processing happens locally in your web browser using modern web technologies.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we may collect anonymous usage data through services like Google Analytics, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring websites
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • General geographic location (country level)

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

How We Use Information

  • To improve our tools and user experience
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To respond to customer support inquiries

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics - For website analytics
  • Google AdSense - For advertising
  • Vercel - For website hosting

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@photocompressorpro.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
