About PhotoCompressorPro

Free, privacy-focused online tools for all your image needs

Our Mission

PhotoCompressorPro was created to provide free, high-quality image tools that respect your privacy. Unlike many online services, our tools work directly in your browser - no image uploads to external servers required.

Why Choose Our Tools?

  • Complete Privacy - Your images never leave your computer
  • 100% Free - No hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations
  • Fast Processing - Works directly in your browser
  • No Registration - Start using immediately
  • Cross-Platform - Works on all modern browsers and devices

Our Tools

  • Image Compressor - Reduce file size without losing quality
  • Image to PDF Converter - Convert and merge images to PDF
  • Background Remover - Remove backgrounds from images

Technology

All our tools use modern web technologies (HTML5, Canvas API, JavaScript) to process your images locally in your browser. This ensures maximum privacy and security for your files.
